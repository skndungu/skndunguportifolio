Building it...

Smart Alarm is a security device that find applications in homes, office and business premises, It uses shock sensors that are places on strategic locations that are likely to suffer intrusion, once a vibration is detected, the alarm makes a call to the owner and any other relevant person, it also sets an alarm siren. However you can still make a call to your smart device and hence set the alarm on.

Technology

This is also an almost fully Mechatronic system, it comprises of the mechanical, electrical & electronics and the software part. The mechanical part is designed using SolidWorks CAD sofware, its PCB is designed on Eagle cadsoft while its firmware is based on the Arduino language. The mechanical sections offers housing for the components as well as mounting sections. The firmware drives all the key functions expected.
